Video Game Review



Astro's Playroom

PS5

Released: 2020

Developed by: Japan Studio (Team Asobi)



Astro's Playroom, developed by Japan Studio's Team ASOBI and published by Sony Interactive Entertainment, is a 3D platformer game that comes pre-installed on the PlayStation 5. As a spiritual successor to Astro Bot Rescue Mission, Astro's Playroom offers charming gameplay and serves as a showcase for the capabilities of the new PlayStation 5 hardware, particularly the DualSense controller. In this review, I will discuss various aspects of the game, including the story, gameplay, graphics, and sound.


Story

In Astro's Playroom, players control Astro, a lovable robot, as they explore various areas within the PlayStation 5 console. The game's narrative is light-hearted and filled with nostalgic references to the history of PlayStation, paying homage to its consoles, accessories, and iconic characters. While the story may be minimalistic, the joyful and celebratory tone of the game keeps players engaged and entertained throughout.


Gameplay

Astro's Playroom features classic 3D platforming gameplay, with players guiding Astro through colorful environments filled with obstacles, enemies, and collectibles. The game's controls are tight and responsive, making navigating the levels a satisfying experience.


One of the standout aspects of Astro's Playroom is its utilization of the PlayStation 5's DualSense controller. The game showcases the controller's haptic feedback, adaptive triggers, and motion controls, providing players with a tactile and immersive experience. From feeling the subtle textures of surfaces beneath Astro's feet to the tension of pulling a bowstring, the game truly demonstrates the potential of the DualSense controller.


The game is divided into four main worlds, each representing a different component of the PlayStation 5. As players explore these worlds, they will encounter various puzzles and challenges that take advantage of the DualSense controller's unique features.


Graphics

Astro's Playroom showcases the power of the PlayStation 5's graphical capabilities, with vibrant and detailed visuals that bring the game's environments to life. The game's art style is charming and whimsical, with expressive animations and delightful character designs. The impressive lighting and particle effects further enhance the game's visual appeal, making Astro's Playroom a feast for the eyes.


Sound

The game features a catchy and upbeat soundtrack composed by Kenneth C M Young, which complements the lighthearted atmosphere of Astro's Playroom. The sound design is also excellent, with each action and environment having distinct and fitting sound effects. The game makes good use of the DualSense controller's built-in speaker, providing players with additional layers of audio feedback that enhance the overall experience.


Conclusion

Astro's Playroom is a delightful 3D platformer that serves as both an engaging game and a showcase for the PlayStation 5's capabilities. With its charming visuals, innovative use of the DualSense controller, and nostalgic celebration of PlayStation's history, the game offers a memorable experience for players of all ages. While it may not be the most expansive or challenging platformer, Astro's Playroom is a must-play title for anyone with a PlayStation 5, providing a joyful and immersive introduction to the potential of the new console.
