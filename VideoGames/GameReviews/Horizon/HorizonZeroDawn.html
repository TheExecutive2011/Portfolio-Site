Video Game Review



Horizon Zero Dawn

PlayStation 4

Released: 2017

Developed by: Guerrilla Games



Horizon Zero Dawn, developed by Guerrilla Games and published by Sony Interactive Entertainment, is an action-adventure game with role-playing elements, initially released in 2017 for the PlayStation 4 and later on PC. As a new intellectual property in the crowded gaming landscape, Horizon Zero Dawn garnered significant attention for its unique premise and stunning visuals. In this review, I will discuss various aspects of the game, including the story, gameplay, graphics, and sound.


Story

Horizon Zero Dawn is set in a post-apocalyptic world inhabited by mysterious robotic creatures known as "machines." Players assume the role of Aloy, a skilled hunter and archer, who has been shunned by her tribe since birth. Aloy embarks on a journey to uncover her origins and the truth behind the machines' existence. The game's narrative is expertly crafted, with a rich and engaging story that keeps players invested in Aloy's journey and the world's lore.


Gameplay

Horizon Zero Dawn offers a vast open-world experience filled with diverse landscapes, unique wildlife, and engaging side quests. The game's core gameplay revolves around hunting machines and using their components to craft weapons, ammunition, and equipment. Each machine has its own distinct behavior patterns and vulnerabilities, requiring players to strategize and adapt their approach accordingly.


The game's combat is both fluid and satisfying, with a variety of weapons and tools at Aloy's disposal. Players can use bows, traps, and a versatile tool called the Ropecaster to disable or defeat machines, while also employing stealth and parkour elements to navigate the environment.


Horizon Zero Dawn also features a skill tree system, allowing players to unlock new abilities and enhance Aloy's combat prowess, stealth, and resource gathering skills. The game's RPG elements, such as crafting, leveling up, and skill progression, add depth and customization to the gameplay experience.


Graphics

Horizon Zero Dawn boasts stunning graphics that bring its post-apocalyptic world to life. The game's lush environments, intricate character models, and breathtaking vistas showcase the power of the Decima engine and the attention to detail from Guerrilla Games. From dense forests to snow-capped mountains, each landscape is a visual treat, immersing players in the game's rich world.


Sound

The game features a captivating soundtrack composed by Joris de Man, The Flight, and others, which perfectly complements the game's atmosphere and narrative. The music effectively conveys the emotions and tone of each scene, while the sound design is top-notch, with realistic environmental audio and the unique sounds of each machine, adding to the game's immersion. Voice acting is also well-executed, with a standout performance from Ashly Burch as Aloy.


Conclusion

Horizon Zero Dawn is a remarkable achievement in the action-adventure genre, offering an engaging story, satisfying gameplay, breathtaking visuals, and immersive sound design. The game's unique setting and captivating narrative make it a must-play for fans of open-world games and action-RPGs. Whether you're exploring the vast landscapes, engaging in tense machine battles, or uncovering the game's deep lore, Horizon Zero Dawn is an unforgettable experience that should not be missed.
