Your Next Steps


Disc golf can be as laid-back or competitive as you want it to be.


Want a competitive experience? Try participating in a local league or tournament! If you're in the Charlotte area, check out the Charlotte Disc Golf Club for more information.


Looking for a fun, laid-back experience? Find a local course and go have fun! My favorite site to find a new course is Disc Golf Course Review. The site might look a bit outdated, but its usefulness certainly is not.
