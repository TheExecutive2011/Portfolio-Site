Board Gamer


Roll the dice!


From playing Monopoly as a child to playing Risk as a college student, I have always loved playing board games. Today, however, the board game industry has evolved and we're in a new golden age for the industry. Fantastic games like 7 Wonders, Betrayal at House on the Hill, Gloomhaven, Kingdomino, Patchwork, Love Letter, and Sushi Go are much more common today than the classics that many of us grew up with.


As I enjoy board games in this new golden age, I'd like to share my thoughts with you on the games that I've played. I am, however, always looking for new games to play. If you've enjoyed a board game lately, tell me about it!
