Vinyl Review



Cheek to Cheek

Tony Bennett & Lady Gaga

Released: 2014

Produced by: Dae Bennett



As a devoted music aficionado, I was eager to experience Tony Bennett and Lady Gaga's collaborative album, "Cheek to Cheek," on vinyl. Released in 2014, this delightful collection of jazz standards brings together the vocal talents of the legendary Bennett and the versatile Gaga, creating a unique and captivating listening experience. Produced by Dae Bennett, the album showcases the duo's exceptional chemistry and their shared passion for jazz music.


"Cheek to Cheek" opens with the iconic "Anything Goes," a Cole Porter classic that sets the tone for the rest of the album. The track features a lively big band arrangement, with Bennett and Gaga's playful, energetic vocals perfectly capturing the spirit of the song.


One of the standout tracks is the album's title song, "Cheek to Cheek." Originally composed by Irving Berlin, this timeless standard is brought to life by the duo's seamless harmonies and heartfelt vocal performances. The song's lush instrumentation and elegant arrangement make it a memorable and enchanting addition to the album.


"Lush Life," a solo performance by Gaga, showcases her ability to fully immerse herself in the world of jazz. Her emotive, soulful vocals convey the song's poignant lyrics with grace and sincerity, revealing a side of the artist that may surprise those more familiar with her pop persona.


"Nature Boy," a touching duet featuring a beautifully restrained orchestral arrangement, highlights the duo's ability to convey deep emotion through their shared vocal talents. Bennett and Gaga's tender harmonies and heartfelt delivery create an intimate atmosphere that draws the listener in.


The vinyl edition of "Cheek to Cheek" offers a warm, authentic listening experience that brings out the best in the album's timeless jazz standards and the duo's exceptional vocal performances. The depth and richness of the analog format perfectly complement the album's lush instrumentation and the captivating chemistry between Bennett and Gaga, making it a must-have for fans and collectors of jazz music.


Final Verdict: "Cheek to Cheek" is a captivating jazz collaboration that showcases the exceptional vocal talents of Tony Bennett and Lady Gaga, featuring timeless jazz standards brought to life by the duo's chemistry and shared passion for the genre. The vinyl edition provides an authentic and enhanced listening experience that captures the essence of the album's lush arrangements and captivating performances, making it a must-listen for fans of jazz and admirers of both artists.
