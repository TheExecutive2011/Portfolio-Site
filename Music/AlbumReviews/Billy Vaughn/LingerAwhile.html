Vinyl Review



Linger Awhile

Billy Vaughn

Released: 1960

Produced by: Randy Wood



As a music enthusiast with a penchant for easy listening and orchestral pop, I eagerly delved into "Linger Awhile" by Billy Vaughn on vinyl. Released in 1958, this delightful album features a collection of instrumental tunes that showcase Vaughn's talent as a bandleader, arranger, and composer. With a soothing blend of lush orchestral arrangements and smooth melodies, "Linger Awhile" transports listeners back to the golden age of easy listening.


The album opens with the charming title track, "Linger Awhile," setting the tone for the rest of the record with its dreamy, relaxing melody. The song's gentle tempo and warm orchestration create a welcoming atmosphere that invites listeners to sit back and immerse themselves in the music.


One standout track on the album is the enchanting "Carolina Moon." With its nostalgic melody and rich, romantic string arrangements, this tune effortlessly captures the essence of the late 1950s and showcases Vaughn's skill as an arranger.
