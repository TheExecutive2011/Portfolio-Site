Chronological Backstory

Early Life

[Insert name], a Shadar-kai commoner, was born into a world of poverty and oppression. His parents were hard-working laborers, but their toil provided little relief from the misery that surrounded them. Shadowfell was a place of darkness and death, where the powerful and corrupt ruled over the weak and defenseless.


Driven to Lead/Rebel/Overthrow the Establishment

From an early age, the boy knew that he was different from the other Shadar-kai. While they accepted their fate with resignation, he burned with a fierce desire to change the world around him. He saw the injustice and cruelty that pervaded Shadowfell and swore to do everything in his power to overthrow the ruling class.


Study of Shadow Magic

As he grew older, the boy became increasingly obsessed with the study of shadow magic. He believed that this arcane discipline held the key to his dreams of revolution and equality. He spent hours poring over ancient tomes and experimenting with powerful spells, driven by an unshakable determination to bring about change.


Repeated Failure

Despite his best efforts, however, the boy's attempts to undermine the ruling class always ended in failure. The powerful and corrupt were too entrenched in their positions of power, and their grip on the people of Shadowfell was too strong to be broken by any one person.


Desperation

Desperate for a breakthrough, the boy began to scour the ruins and dark corners of Shadowfell in search of a legendary artifact. He had heard whispers of its existence from the old crones and wise men of his village, and he believed that it held the key to his success.


Finding the Legendary Artifact

Years passed, and the boy grew into a young man. He became more skilled in the ways of magic, but still, his attempts to overthrow the ruling class came to nothing. He was on the verge of despair when he finally found what he had been looking for - the legendary artifact that was said to provide unstoppable abilities and strength.


Betrayal/Not as the Legend Told.../Fooled by Legends

The boy's heart pounded with excitement as he reached out to touch the artifact. He could feel its power coursing through his veins, and he knew that victory was finally within his grasp. But as he stepped through the portal that opened before him, he realized with horror that the artifact was not as he had believed it to be.


The Material Plane

The portal deposited him in the material plane, far from the dark and twisted landscape of Shadowfell. He looked around in confusion, realizing that he had been duped by the legends that he had believed for so long. He had no unstoppable abilities or strength - only the same magic that he had always possessed.


Opportunity

As he wandered through the material plane, Merrick Ravenfall began to realize that this new world held many wonders and opportunities that were impossible in Shadowfell. He found that the people here were different, too - they were full of hope and life, rather than the despair and resignation that he had known all his life.


Learning in a Brand New World

Merrick Ravenfall decided to stay in the material plane, determined to make a new life for himself. He knew that he could never forget his dreams of revolution and equality, but he also understood that he needed to find a new way to achieve them. He began to explore his magical abilities in new ways, experimenting with spells that he had never tried before.


The Search for Power

As he traveled across the land, he encountered a group of adventurers who had been hired to investigate a series of disappearances in a nearby town. Merrick Ravenfall was intrigued by their mission, and he offered to lend his magical talents to their cause. The other adventurers were wary of this stranger at first, but they quickly realized that he was a valuable ally. Merrick Ravenfall's magic helped them to unravel the mystery of the disappearances, and they soon became fast friends.
