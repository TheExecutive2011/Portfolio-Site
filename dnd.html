Dungeons & Dragons


...and other tabletop RPGs!


I play Dungeons & Dragons. And Cyberpunk 2020. And Dread. And Weird Frontiers.


If it's an RPG, I'll play it! Whether it's with friends or people who will soon be friends, an RPG night is sure to be a memorable one. Anything is possible in these worlds of wonder and awe!


This hobby also sparked an obsession with dice sets. Do you have an obsession with dice, too? Let me know!


This page is still in development, though. Once it is finished, it will be designed to provide you insights into the campaigns that I'm currently working on, along with highlights from past D&D shenanigans.


In the meantime, check out my character sheet from a recent one-shot playthrough. The stats are a mess, but this build was an absolute blast to play with!
