Backstory

Rockerboy

Rockerboys are rebellious musicians who use music and revolt to fight authority. They are a lot like '80s punk rockers who look down on corporate "sellouts" as the traitors to teh craft. Because they are so charismatic, they can sway, incitea, and charm a large number of people through musical performances.
