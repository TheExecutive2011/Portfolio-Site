Backstory

Thief

Bobbert's goal was to become the greatest thief that ever lived. He constantly scans his surroundings. If there is anything of value, Bobbert will probably attempt to steal it.

Tools

Dice Set

Bobbert is proficient with a dice set, and can add his proficiency bonus to ability checks made to play a game with that set.


Poisoner's Kit

This small box contains a variety of papers and parchments, pens and inks, seals and sealing wax, gold and silver leaf, and other supplies necessary to create convincing forgeries of physical documents. Bobbert is proficient with this kit, allowing him to add his proficiency bonus to any ability checks made to create a physical forgery of a document.


Thieves' Tools

This set of tools includes a small file, a set of lock picks, a small mirror mounted on a metal handle, a set of narrow-bladed scissors, and a pair of pliers. Bobbert is proficiency with these tools, allowing him to add his proficiency bonus to any ability checks made to disarm traps or open locks.


Languages and Communication

Common

Common is a language spoken by most races and creatures in D&D. Bobbert's native language is Common.


Elvish

The language of elves and most elven subraces.


Thieves' Cant

A secret mix of dialect, jargon, and code that allows messages to be hidden in seemingly normal conversation. Only another creature that knows thieves' cant understands such messages. It takes four times longer to convey such a message than it does to speak the same idea plainly.


Undercommon

A secret language that uses the Elvish script, but shares little more. It is typically spoken by creatures that live underground.
